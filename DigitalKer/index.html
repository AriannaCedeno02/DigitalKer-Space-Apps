Introduction

1Project

The Digital KER team, made up of FIEC 5th year students, conducting studies on NASA, will present a project based on the contributions of NASA and its discoveries over 6 decades. Artificial Intelligence techniques will also be implemented for the development of this project.

2NASA

NASA worldwide is a highly recognized institution that has contributed to the development of Astrology worldwide, with the study of celestial bodies and everything related to space, that is why this institution, with 62 years of experience, service to our Planet Earth, we will create a web page where we will show their discoveries and contributions in astrological matters in our universe.

3NASA in the future

Technological development has already begun to enable a manned mission to Mars in the 2030s. Many of the capabilities will first be demonstrated on the Moon, during the Artemis missions, while other systems are better suited for deeper spaces.
